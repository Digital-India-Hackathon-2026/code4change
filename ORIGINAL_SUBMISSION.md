## Idea
SkillSwap Mesh is an app that lets people trade skills instead of money — like a marriage broker, but for skills. Every neighborhood has hidden talent sitting unused: someone who can cook, someone who plays guitar, someone who's good with computers. Instead of paying for classes, people list what they can teach and what they want to learn, and the app matches them up.The clever part is that it doesn't just pair two people directly — it finds hidden three-way (or more) circles, like A teaches B, B teaches C, and C teaches A, so everyone gets to learn something even if no two people match perfectly
